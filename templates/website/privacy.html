Emati Privacy Policy

Emati is a personalized service, which screens scientific articles to recommend those, which match the user's interest.

To deliver and improve the service, Emati stores the following personal information:

  • Basics subscriber information
    • Name, email, link to social profile (Google and Facebook). Facebook profile information (when you use Facebook to log-in to or to create an account on Emati) Goole profile information (when you use Google to log-in to or to create an account on Emati) This data is needed for the registration and notification purposes.
  • Usage data
    • Uploaded files, selected articles, time stamps, log in events needed to maintain profile, profile aging, user specific search criteria. Information derived from IP addresses such as country or institution is used for internal purposes and will not be disclosed with third parties. A user can delete his profile himself or request for it's deletion. Profile deletion option is a part of web interface.
  • Server log files
    • IP addresses and urls are logged to maintain the availability and security of this site, and to detect and correct errors in our IT systems. Log files will be deleted after 7 days.
  • Cookies
    • Emati is using cookies to maintain better user expirience. You can get more information about cookies here
  • Automatic account removal
    • Accounts inactive for more than 6 month will be automatically deleted with all their data. Further more the Data Protection Declaration of the TU Dresden applies. {% endwith %} {% endwith %}
